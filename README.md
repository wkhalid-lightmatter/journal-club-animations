# Journal Club — Interactive Animations

Interactive physics simulations for the weekly journal club focused on lasers and SOAs.

## Animations

### [Feedback Regime Explorer](index.html)
**Dong et al., Optica 12(8), 1171 (2025)**

Drag the slider or press Play to sweep secondary optical feedback strength η_F and watch:
- The Lorentzian optical spectrum narrow or collapse (with live FWHM = Δν bracket)
- The frequency noise floor track the linewidth (Δν = π · S_ν⁰)
- The output intensity transition from stable → chaotic

Devices: QW DFB · E-DBR · 4 m ECL

### [Mode-Hopping vs. Broadband Locking Simulator](mode-hopping-simulator.html)
Interactive demo showing why broadband DBR reflection prevents mode-hopping compared to a narrow high-Q microresonator.
